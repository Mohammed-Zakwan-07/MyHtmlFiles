1.ROSE

Roses are among the most popular and widely cultivated flowers in the world.They are known for their beauty, fragrance, and symbolic meanings. They belong to the Rosa genus and are native to a variety of regions.

2.SUNFLOWER

Sunflowers (Helianthus annuus) are iconic, bright yellow flowers known for their large blooms and the way they turn to face the sun.Sunflowers have become a symbol of warmth, positivity, and longevity.

3.HIBISCUS

Hibiscus is a flowering plant known for its large, colorful blooms and is part of the Malvaceae family. Native to warm, tropical and subtropical regions, hibiscus flowers are popular for their aesthetic appeal and their use in teas, food.

4.MARIGOLD

Marigolds (Tagetes spp.) are cheerful, hardy flowers known for their vibrant orange, yellow, and red blooms. They are widely appreciated for their beauty, ease of growth, and various uses in gardening and traditional practices.

5.JASMINE

Jasmine (Jasminum spp.) is a fragrant flowering plant known for its small, star-shaped blossoms and its intoxicating, sweet scent. It is widely appreciated for its beauty, symbolic meanings, and uses in various cultural and medicinal.

6.DAISY

The daisy (Bellis perennis) is a cheerful, classic flower with a simple charm and widespread popularity in gardens and wildflower meadows. Known for its symbolic meanings and versatile uses, the daisy is appreciated worldwide.

7.TULIP

Tulips (Tulipa spp.) are one of the most well-known and beloved spring flowers, recognized for their vibrant colors and elegant, cup-shaped blooms. Originating from Central Asia and cultivated widely, they have become a symbol of beauty and rebirth.

8.LILY

The lily (Lilium spp.) is a highly valued and striking flower known for its large, colorful blooms and often sweet fragrance. With its unique structure and extensive symbolism, the lily is beloved in gardens and ceremonies around the world.

9.ORCHID

Orchids are a diverse and fascinating group of flowering plants belonging to the family Orchidaceae, which is one of the largest families of flowering plants. Known for their unique and intricate flowers, orchids have captivated plant lovers around the world.

10.DAFFODIL

Daffodils (Narcissus spp.) are popular spring flowers known for their bright, cheerful blooms and pleasant fragrance. They are often among the first flowers to bloom in the spring, bringing vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.

11.LAVENDER

Lavender (Lavandula spp.) is a popular aromatic herb known for its vibrant purple flowers and soothing fragrance. Widely cultivated for its beauty and numerous uses, lavender is cherished in gardens, cooking, and traditional medicine.

12.CHRYSANTHEMUM

Chrysanthemums, commonly known as mums, are vibrant, flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae. Known for their diverse colors and shapes, chrysanthemums are popular in gardens, floral arrangements, and as potted plants.

13.PEONY

Peonies (Paeonia spp.) are beloved perennial plants known for their lush, fragrant, and vibrant flowers. Native to Asia, Europe, and North America, peonies have been cultivated for thousands of years and are prized for their large, showy blooms and hardy nature.

14.IRIS

Irises (Iris spp.) are striking and versatile flowers admired for their bold colors and unique shape. Named after the Greek goddess of the rainbow, irises come in a wide range of hues and are known for their sword-shaped leaves and distinctive, elegant flowers.

15.POPPY

Poppies (Papaver spp.) are iconic flowering plants admired for their bright colors and delicate, crinkly petals. They have been cultivated for thousands of years and hold rich symbolic, medicinal, and culinary significance.
